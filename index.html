SentinelMesh

AI-powered civic safety mesh for schools, campuses, and smart cities.

What is SentinelMesh?

SentinelMesh deploys lightweight AI nodes across buildings, campuses, and city zones to detect threats, unusual patterns, and risks in real time — without storing personal data.

Why Pilots?

Our early access program partners with schools, councils, and public operators to test real-time alerts, zone-level detection, and automated response flows.

Who Can Participate?

Schools, local government, public buildings, campuses, retail hubs, and infrastructure operators.
